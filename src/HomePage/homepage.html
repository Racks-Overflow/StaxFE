StaxOverFlow

This is where your next group adventure begins!

We are here to help you create priceless moments during the trip of your dreams. We do this by helping you set, track, and accomplish a savings goal to share with a group. Once the fun begins you all can relax and know your money is ready to be spent effortlessly on the things that matter most. The steps are pretty simple:

  1. Get the boring stuff out of the way and create your account.
  2. Use our build-a-trip questionaire to get an estimate for your next trip. Just an estimate - we don't have a crystal ball...yet.
  3. Invite everyone! Ok...maybe not everyone but there has to be someone willing to spend time with you.
  4. Save, save, save! Everyone contributing to the same goal in equal amounts; we like things fair and square.
  5. Then once you start your trip the savings account becomes a spend account that you, and whatever poor soul you dragged along, can use to pay for group activities with!
No more sending money back and forth with 4-5 different apps -cause everyone always has a different one- OR forcing your poor waiter to split the bill! Pay the tab and keep the fun going!

Pro tip! If you want you can take a look at our build-a-trip questionaire without signing in.
